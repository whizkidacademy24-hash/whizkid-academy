Fundamental Framework

Six modules built around how markets actually move — driven by central banks, global economics, and geopolitical forces that most retail traders completely ignore.

Module 01
Central Bank Policy

Decode Fed, ECB, and BOJ decisions. Learn to read rate cycles and understand how monetary policy drives forex and equities before the move happens.

📊
Module 02
Macroeconomics

GDP, inflation, employment — learn to interpret economic releases the way institutional desks do, and position ahead of consensus.

How global events create tradeable dislocations across forex, commodities, and indices — and how to identify them early.
